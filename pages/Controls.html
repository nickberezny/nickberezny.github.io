Robustness Framework for Impedance/Admittance Control
Interaction controllers are typically viewed from the perspective of causality: Impedance control is a torque-based controller, whereas Admittance control is a position-based controller. In this work I reframe interaction control in terms or robustness to modelling error and force sensor error. Unlike in trajectory trakcing, there is a robustness trade-off.
